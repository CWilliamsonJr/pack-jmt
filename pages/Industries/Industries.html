How Surveys improve Health Care

We provide excellence resource for patients and providers. The surveys are to bring patients and providers together as concerns are issued. We conducted a study that showed 93% of patients want to know information about their providers’ education and training, but only 60% of healthcare organizations provide it. Online surveys support patient and providers’ understandings by getting feedback from each other. A lot of clinics, hopitals, and other private providers have provided feedbacks:

• 75% use surveys to detect patients’ health habits
• More than half use surveys to monitor employee satifaction
